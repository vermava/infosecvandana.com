Jeff Moss  |  Former Chief Security Officer and VP at ICANN, Founder of Black Hat and DEF CON Conferences, U.S. Department of Homeland Security Advisory Council

Ryan Flores  |  Senior Manager, Forward-Looking Threat Research Team APAC

Vandana Verma Sehgal  |  Security Relations Leader, Snyk

Date: Thursday, May 12 | 4:20pm-5:00pm ( Virtual & Roselle Junior Ballroom 4610 )

Format: 40-Minute Briefings

Track: Keynote

Join Black Hat Asia Review Board members Ryan Flores, Lidia Giuliano, Vandana Verma, and Stefano Zanero for an insightful conversation on the most pressing issues facing the InfoSec community, moderated by Jeff Moss. This Locknote will feature a candid discussion on the key takeaways coming out of the conference and how these trends will impact future InfoSec strategies.
