**Panel: Hacking the Status Quo - Tales From Leading Women in Cybersecurity**

Panel at Black Hat USA 2025 featuring leading women from the Black Hat USA Review Board. The conversation shared journeys, challenges, and triumphs across cybersecurity, with a focus on research, leadership, and breaking long-standing norms in the industry.

Themes discussed:
- Personal paths into security research and leadership
- Navigating bias and visibility in offensive and defensive security
- Sustaining influence and community beyond the conference stage
- Advice for the next generation of women entering cybersecurity
