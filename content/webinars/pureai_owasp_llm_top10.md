**OWASP Top 10 for LLMs**

Webcast presented via PureAI as a Snyk expert and OWASP leader, walking through the OWASP Top 10 for Large Language Model Applications. The session covered each of the top vulnerabilities, how they manifest in real AI-assisted development workflows, and shared actionable strategies for securing AI-native applications in the enterprise.

Topics covered:
- Prompt injection and insecure output handling
- Training data poisoning and supply chain risks for LLMs
- Model denial of service and excessive agency
- Secure patterns for LLM-integrated applications
- How the OWASP Top 10 for LLM project maps to real developer workflows
