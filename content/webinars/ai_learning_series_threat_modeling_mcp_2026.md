**Securing the Invisible Thread: Threat Modeling and Defending MCP in Agentic AI Systems**

This AI Learning Series session by Vandana Verma explores how MCP connects LLMs to tools, APIs, data, and systems, quietly reshaping trust boundaries and expanding the attack surface beyond what traditional AppSec models were built for.

As agentic AI adoption accelerates, the session focuses on how MCP can turn well-intentioned agents into effective attack orchestrators through:

- context poisoning
- token and secret leakage
- privilege escalation via scope creep
- tool misuse
- shadow MCP servers

The goal of the session is not to slow AI innovation, but to secure how power flows across agentic systems.
