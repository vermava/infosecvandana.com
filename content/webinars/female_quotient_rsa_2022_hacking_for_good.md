The Female Quotient hosted this Equality Lounge conversation at RSA Conference on the idea of "hacking for good" and why women with cybersecurity and hacker mindsets are essential to the future of the industry.

The public event description highlights Vandana Verma alongside Deloitte, Chloé Messdaghi, Cybrary, Jax S., and Outpost Gray in a discussion about the purpose behind ethical hacking and the importance of visible women leaders in this work.
