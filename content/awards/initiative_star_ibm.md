Initiative Star Contributor Award - Issued by IBM

Member or non-member of the IBM Academy of Technology who has completed 3+ Academy Initiatives since January 1, 2020 and at least one must have been within the 12 months prior to the badge request, with a "Satisfactory" rating or better and participant rating of "Rock Star" or "Strong Participation" for those initiatives.
