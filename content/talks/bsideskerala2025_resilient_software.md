**Panel: Building Resilient Software**

Panel discussion at BSides Kerala 2025 exploring the critical intersection of Product Security, DevSecOps, and Application Security (AppSec). The conversation focused on how teams can integrate security throughout the software development lifecycle, balance agility with risk management, and build products that are resilient by design rather than by patch.

Topics covered included:
- Shifting security left without blocking developer velocity
- Practical product security practices at scale
- Balancing speed, risk, and compliance in modern delivery pipelines
- Lessons learned from incidents and response
