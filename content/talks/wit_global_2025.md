**Session: Implementing Zero Trust in an Organisation to be Uncertainty Ready**

Session at the Women in Tech Global Conference 2025 exploring how organisations can operationalise Zero Trust to stay resilient in uncertain times. The talk walked through the principles of Zero Trust, common pitfalls in adoption, and a practical roadmap for security and identity teams.

Key takeaways:
- Zero Trust as an operating model, not a product
- Identity, device, and workload as the new perimeter
- How to incrementally roll out Zero Trust without breaking the business
- Making Zero Trust investments audit-ready and uncertainty-ready
