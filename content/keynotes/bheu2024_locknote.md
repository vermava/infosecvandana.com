**Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways from Black Hat Europe 2024 **

Closing locknote panel at Black Hat Europe 2024. Joined conference founder Jeff Moss and members of the Black Hat Review Board to discuss the most significant research, themes, and takeaways from the event. Artificial intelligence - the reality versus the hype - dominated much of the discussion, alongside governance, risk, and compliance themes.
