This public LinkedIn post is a useful reference for Vandana Verma's current AI governance work.

The post explains how three frameworks work together rather than compete:

- `EU AI Act` as the regulatory guardrail
- `ISO/IEC 42001` as the organizational governance layer
- `NIST AI RMF` as the operational risk management playbook
