Founder & President, InfosecGirls (2016 – Present)

Since 2016, InfosecGirls has been more than just a community—it has been a movement to make cybersecurity more inclusive, accessible, and welcoming for everyone. As Founder & President, I have focused on creating a space where people can learn, grow, and build confidence without barriers or labels.

Over the years, we’ve brought together students, professionals, and leaders from across the industry—through workshops, mentorship programs, community sessions, and global collaborations. The goal has always been simple: to open doors, share knowledge, and help individuals find their place in cybersecurity.

What started as a small initiative has grown into a strong, supportive network—driven by the belief that when more voices are included, the entire security ecosystem becomes stronger.

At its heart, InfosecGirls is about empowerment, opportunity, and building a future where talent speaks louder than anything else.
