Vandana Verma co-authored this white paper with contributors from GitLab, Sysdig, and Snyk to help security teams better understand ransomware threats and defend the infrastructure, applications, and people most vulnerable to attack.

The paper focuses on developing a stronger ransomware mindset through cloud-native security patterns and practical collaboration across the ecosystem.
